Frites¶


FRITES = Framework for Information Theoretical analysis of Electrophysiological data and Statistics

Frites is a Python toolbox for assessing information-theorical measures on humain and animal neurophysiological data (M/EEG, Intracranial). The toolbox also includes options for the evaluation of statistics. Significant effects can be found on a specific population of subjects (fixed-effect) or be generalized to a random population (random-effect).
